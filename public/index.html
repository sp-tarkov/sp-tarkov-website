Single Player Tarkov

Experience True Single Player Progression

Single Player Tarkov (SPT) transforms your Escape From Tarkov (EFT) client into a fully offline experience—no more server downtime, cheaters, or forced PvP. Enjoy realistic raids, quest progression, and steady character development on your own terms.

  • Offline Solo Play – Experience all the maps, weapons, and quests of EFT without relying on official servers.
  • Customizable Modding – Customize almost anything; from big updates that enhance AI, gear availability, and new weapons, to smaller quality-of-life tweaks you never knew you needed. Make it yours.
  • Stress-Free Raids – Train skills at your own pace, experiment with loadouts, or tackle quest lines without the pressure of competing players.

Take control of your Tarkov experience. Raid when you want, how you want.
